Research Interest: Cybersecurity, IoT Security, Vulnerability Research, Binary Code Fingerprinting and Malware Analysis, Network Security, Machine Learning.
I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Ryerson University. I joined Rayerson University from an NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowship posistion at Carnegie Mellon University. I hold a PhD degree in Information System Engineering from Concordia University under the suppervison of Dr. Mourad Debbabi and Dr. Lingyu Wang.

I am committed to providing a training and reserach environment built on the fundamental values of Equity, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI). I have multiple funded positions at the Undergrad, Masters, Ph.D., and Postdoctoral levels. I am looking for motivated candidates with strong academic background and technical skills in cybersecurity and/or machine learning. To apply, please email me your CV and your transcripts. You will only hear from me, if I am interested in your application. Please consult the following resources for more information: Computer Science Graduate Program, Admission Requirments, Application Process, and International Applicants.
